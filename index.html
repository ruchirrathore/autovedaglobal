About Us

AutoVeda Global is a dedicated export platform specializing in the procurement, quality assurance, and logistics of premium aftermarket automotive parts, industrial consumables, and precision sensors.

We bridge the gap between top-tier manufacturing facilities in India and high-demand industrial sectors across East Africa, Southeast Asia, and global emerging markets.

Why Global Buyers Partner With Us

  • Verified Export Entity: Fully DGFT & IEC Compliant operation.
  • Cross-Border Logistics: Seamless international freight and customs coordination.
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous pre-shipment inspection protocols for harsh environments.
  • Supply Chain Focus: Dedicated to keeping heavy-duty fleets and industrial networks uninterrupted.

Product Catalog

We facilitate the export of premium aftermarket automotive components and advanced industrial technology. Our global supply chain focuses on the following primary categories:

  • Heavy-Duty Brake Systems: Commercial-grade pads, rotors, and friction materials engineered for high heat-tolerance and heavy fleet operations.
  • Suspension & Steering: Heavy-duty control arms, tie rods, and shock absorbers built for rough terrain and maximum longevity.
  • Industrial Sensors & Electronics: Precision diagnostic and operational sensors for automotive and industrial applications, optimized for reliability in high-humidity environments.
  • Automotive Consumables: Industrial-grade lubricants, specialized adhesives, sealants, and coolants.
