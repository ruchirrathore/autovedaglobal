About Us

AutoVeda Global is a dedicated export firm specializing in the procurement, quality assurance, and logistics of premium aftermarket automotive parts, industrial consumables, and precision sensors. We bridge the gap between top-tier manufacturing facilities in India and high-demand industrial sectors across East Africa, Southeast Asia, and global emerging markets.

Our operational model focuses strictly on reliability, rapid deployment, and rigorous compliance with international cross-border trade regulations. Whether navigating rough terrain or high-humidity tropical climates, we ensure our B2B partners, heavy-duty fleet managers, and retail networks maintain uninterrupted supply chains.

Product Catalog

We facilitate the export of premium aftermarket automotive components and advanced industrial technology. Our global supply chain focuses on the following primary categories:

  • Heavy-Duty Brake Systems: Commercial-grade pads, rotors, and friction materials engineered for high heat-tolerance and heavy fleet operations.
  • Suspension & Steering: Heavy-duty control arms, tie rods, and shock absorbers built for rough terrain and maximum longevity.
  • Industrial Sensors & Electronics: Precision diagnostic and operational sensors for automotive and industrial applications, optimized for reliability in high-humidity and high-temperature environments.
  • Automotive Consumables: Industrial-grade lubricants, specialized adhesives, sealants, and coolants.

*Note: Detailed technical specifications, test-kit availability, and volume pricing matrices are available exclusively to verified B2B partners upon direct request.
