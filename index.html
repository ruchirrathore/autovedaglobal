About Us

AutoVeda Global is a dedicated export firm specializing in the procurement, quality assurance, and logistics of premium aftermarket automotive parts and consumables. We bridge the gap between top-tier manufacturing facilities in India and the high-demand automotive sectors across Russia and the broader CIS region.

Our operational model focuses strictly on reliability, rapid deployment, and rigorous compliance with international cross-border trade regulations, ensuring our B2B partners and retail networks receive uninterrupted supply chains.

Product Catalog

We facilitate the export of standard, non-dual-use civilian automotive components. Our current trade corridors focus on the following primary categories:

  • Brake Systems: Pads, rotors, and hydraulic components for commercial and passenger vehicles.
  • Filtration: High-efficiency oil, air, and cabin filters.
  • Suspension & Steering: Control arms, tie rods, and shock absorbers engineered for harsh climates.
  • Automotive Consumables: Industrial-grade lubricants, sealants, and coolants.

*Note: Detailed technical specifications and volume pricing matrices are available exclusively to verified B2B partners upon direct request.
